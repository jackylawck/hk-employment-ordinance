# ⚖️ HK Employment Ordinance (Cap. 57) Compliance Advisor (香港僱傭條例合規工作站)

An enterprise compliance workstation built 100% deterministically per the Hong Kong Employment Ordinance (Cap. 57) and the Labour Department's guidelines.
本系統為一套企業級勞工法例合規工作站，核心邏輯 100% 依據香港法例第 57 章《僱傭條例》及勞工處官方指引進行決定性編碼。

👉 [點此立即訪問線上動態合規風險評分卡系統 (Live Sandbox App)](https://hk-employment-ordinance.streamlit.app/)

---

### ⚠️ 範圍與免責聲明 (Scope and Limitation of Liability)

* **Strict Alignment (嚴格對齊基準)：** This system is deterministically coded based exclusively on the HK Employment Ordinance (Cap. 57) and the Labour Department's Concise Guide.
  本系統核心邏輯 100% 依據香港《僱傭條例》(Cap. 57) 進行決定性編碼。

* **Out of Scope (超出範圍界定)：** It does not evaluate tax, immigration visas, MPF investments, general criminal law, or non-employment related corporate policies.
  本系統不評估稅務、入境簽證、強積金投資策略、一般刑事法，或與勞工法例無關之企業內部政策。

* **Not Legal Advice (非法律意見)：** The outputs generated by this diagnostic engine are for internal HR governance reference and risk triage only. They do not constitute formal legal or professional compliance advice.
  本診斷引擎生成之結果僅供企業內部 HR 管治參考與初步風險定級，絕對不構成正式之法律或專業合規意見。

### 🛡️ 治理設計理念與技術架構 (Governance Philosophy & Technical Architecture)

* **100% Deterministic Rule-based Engine (決定性規則引擎)：** Zero LLM Hallucinations. The system does NOT integrate with any third-party public LLM APIs (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude). All statutory outputs use deterministic logic and Regex keyword matching to ensure complete audit traceability.
  **無 AI 幻覺**：本系統無接入任何第三方公有大語言模型 API。所有法規輸出均採用 100% 決定性邏輯，確保審計軌跡 (Audit Trail) 完全透明可查。

* **Zero-Storage Privacy Framework (數據隱私與零存儲機制)：** No RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) or Vector Databases are used. Zero persistent storage is implemented. All inputs run in a sealed browser session and are completely wiped upon closing or refreshing the page.
  **記憶體即時清空**：本應用採用「無持久性存儲」設計，不設後台數據庫。用戶輸入的情境查詢僅在當下 session 密封運行，網頁關閉即在雲端完全清空歸零，符合國際隱私審計最高標準。

* **Strict Boundary Control (嚴格邊界控制 & 拒絕作答)：** Queries falling outside the scope of Cap. 57 will be explicitly rejected to prevent the creation of "Compliance Illusions".
  **邊界外拒絕作答**：若查詢內容超出《僱傭條例》適用範圍，系統將觸發防護欄 (Guardrails) 拒絕提供推測性建議，嚴防合規幻覺 (Compliance Illusion)。

* **Usage Advice (使用建議)：** Evaluation results are for internal risk assessment and governance workflow reference only. Please consult professional legal counsel or the Labour Department for actual compliance deployment.
  **實務部署**：評估結果僅供初步風險排查。遇重大勞資決策，請務必諮詢專業法律顧問或香港勞工處。
