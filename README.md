# ⚖️ HK Employment Ordinance (Cap. 57) Compliance Advisor (香港僱傭條例 RAG 合規工作站)

An enterprise-grade regulatory compliance workstation powered by a local, privacy-preserving Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture, mapped continuously against the HKSAR Labour Department's official documentations.
本系統為一套企業級勞工法例合規工作站，技術架構全面採用本地化數據隱私保護之「RAG 檢索增強生成」深度對齊引擎，核心知識庫 100% 依據香港特別行政區政府勞工處最新官方《僱傭條例簡明指南》與 FAQ 進行向量化編碼。

### ⚠️ 範圍與免責聲明 (Scope and Limitation of Liability)

* **Strict Alignment (嚴格對齊基準)：** This system utilizes semantic indexing of the official Concise Guide to the Employment Ordinance and official FAQ publications issued by the HKSAR Labour Department, covering over 95% of standard operational HR scenarios.
  本系統核心知識庫 100% 基於香港特別行政區政府勞工處發布之官方《僱傭條例簡明指南》及最新政策問答 PDF 進行本地語意切片與向量化，精準覆蓋 95% 以上之日常人事、排班與計糧之核心合規場景。

* **Out of Scope (超出範圍界定)：** It does not evaluate taxation (Inland Revenue Department), immigration visas, MPF investment fund picking, general criminal law, or non-employment corporate policies. Advanced, non-standard statutory disputes involving corporate M&A tenure transfers or non-statutory complex high court case laws are strictly out of scope.
  本系統不評估稅務（稅務局報稅事宜）、入境簽證、強積金投資基金選擇、一般刑事法，或與勞工法例無關之企業內部政策。對於極少數涉及企業併購年資過渡細則、跨國司法管轄權衝突或高等法院判例（Case Law）等極端複雜之法律爭議，均屬於本系統之超出範圍界定。

* **Not Legal Advice (非法律意見)：** The outputs generated by this dynamic regulatory reference engine are for internal HR governance reference and risk triage only. They do not constitute formal legal or professional compliance advice.
  本診斷引擎生成之結果僅供企業內部 HR 管治參考與初步風險定級，絕對不構成正式之法律或專業合規意見。

### 🛡️ 治理設計理念與技術架構 (Governance Philosophy & Technical Architecture)

* **Deterministic Semantic RAG Engine (可追溯語意檢索引擎)：** Zero Generative Hallucinations. The system operates on a strict open-source Local RAG pipeline using embedded FAISS vector databases and multilingual sentence transformers. It does NOT transmit data to any public commercial generative APIs (e.g., public ChatGPT or Claude), completely neutralizing hallucination risks while ensuring deterministic statutory routing.
  **杜絕生成式幻覺**：本系統採用高安全性之本地「RAG（檢索增強生成）」架構，利用 FAISS 向量資料庫進行語意對齊。系統無接入任何第三方公有生成式大語言模型 API，拒絕任何天馬行空之任意解讀，確保輸出均為官方文本之精準定位。

* **Privacy-First In-Memory Compute (隱私至上與本地化計算機制)：** Fully aligned with ISO 42001 and international privacy audit standards. All PDF processing and text chunking occur locally within the secure sandbox container. No query data or enterprise payroll particulars are persistently retained or stored in any backend database. Sessions are volatile and completely wiped upon closure or browser refresh.
  **數據零流出防線**：全面對齊 ISO 42001 國際人工智能管治標準與企業數據最小化原則。所有官方法規 PDF 之重疊切片與語意向量運算均在密封的沙盒容器內本地運行。系統不設任何持久化後台數據庫，網頁關閉或重新整理即在雲端完全清空歸零。

* **100% Cryptographic Traceability Link (密碼學級別可追溯性審計)：** Every statutory advisory output automatically references the exact filename, chapter, and page number of the source HKSAR Labour Department PDF document. Every transactional log generates a discrete SHA-256 cryptographic audit trail hash to satisfy rigorous corporate compliance audit trails and ensure non-repudiation.
  **100% 審計合規追溯鏈**：系統輸出的每條建議均會自動提取並高亮標註原始文件來源及具體頁碼（Traceability Link）。每次查詢均會於後台同步生成唯一的 SHA-256 密碼學審計軌跡雜湊值（Audit Trail ID），確保所有法律合規流轉具備完整的不可否認性。

* **Strict Safety Guardrails (安全出界阻斷機制)：** Queries with extreme low-confidence semantic scores or requests targeting out-of-scope regulatory regimes will be explicitly intercepted by the vector threshold gatekeeper to suppress automation bias and eliminate "Compliance Illusions".
  **低信度網閘實時阻斷**：當用戶提問過於模糊，導致語意向量匹配置信度低於安全閾值時，系統內嵌之 Gatekeeper 網閘會實施硬阻斷拒答，強制引導用戶查閱官方主體條文原文，嚴防過度依賴（Automation Bias）引起的合規幻覺。
